BURIAL RITUALS

When a Muslim dies, it is the responsibility of his family or other Muslims within the local community to wash and buried him according to the Islamic rites of washing the deceased. Usually at least eight to ten people will be involved with these activities (washing and shrouding).

There are few steps that need to be followed :

1. First Wajib Act: Ghusl Mayyit (Washing the Dead Body) Method;

2. Second Wajib Act: Tahnit or Hunut (Applying Camphor Powder);

3. Third Wajib Act: Kafan;

4. Fourth Wajib Act: Salaatul Mayyit;

5. Fifth Wajib Act: Dafan, Burial
