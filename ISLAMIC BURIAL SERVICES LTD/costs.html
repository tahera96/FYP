Burial costs

The bulk of the fee for a burial conducted by Malaysia government is related to the islamic law and malaysia governments law .
IBS is charged based on location and time which include all pickup & transfer fees, completion of all forms and legislative paperwork,
including the registration of the death, arrangements for the Ghusl (washing), Kaffan (shroud) and finally the Burial.

All proceeds are used to manage and run the service, which operates as a self-sufficient organisation, and constantly strive to improve the facility
or services we manage.
