Praise be to Allah, the Lord of the Worlds; and may His blessings and peace be upon our Prophet Muhammad and upon all his Family and Companions.
In Islam the funeral process is the duty and responsibility of every Muslim community.It is an obligatory act for Muslims. If it is done by atleast one person of the society then the society released from their responsibility but if not then it will considered as sinful for the entire society.
In Malaysia this role has been shared between the local mosque and the Funeral Director. In Islam there are differences between burial of man and woman.Sometimes it is hard to fulfil all the requirements from the family. Islamic Funeral Services Ltd (IFS) was established in 2015 to fulfil the role of a Muslim Funeral Director in Selangor state. We are here to help all the Muslim family to find the right place in the right moment for their loved one.
